Beta Testing

The AT Products Website

General Information

Beta testing is one of AT Products’s most focused things ever due to website code being released to the public. Beta testers get links very update that gets approved by other web developers first.

What you'll be testing out might be Websites, and Discord Bots.

Your Role

Beta testers can optionally open the link from the link provided by the email that we send. Once opened, it would be recommended that you give your opinions by replying to the email. Also look for bugs, and report them by again, replying to the email. Beta Testers have a important role for our website.

How to Apply

To apply as a beta tester, email support@atproducts.org with basically a acceptance. Here’s a example template.

Name: Alyssa Hoffman
Role: Beta Tester
Invitation ID: 509246
Testing: (Discord Bots, Websites, or both!)

Once sent, we will try our best to reply with information when to begin, any delays, or other information.
Every email from then will be a link with a date, or other information.

Hopefully we can have you as a part of AT Products's Beta Testing team!
Note that you may be counted as AT Products's Staff and may be invited to events
